Where to report a suspected asbestos violation

  1. You may submit a tip through an online form on the EPA website at http://www.epa.gov/tips/
  2. Reach out to your state asbestos administrative department and regional asbestos program
    In Missouri:
    • Missouri Department of Natural Resources
    • Missouri EPA
    • St. Louis City Air Pollution Control
    • St. Louis County Air Pollution Control
    In Illinois:
    • Illinois Department of Public Health
    • Illinois EPA
    • Chicago Department of Public Health
  3. If you suspect asbestos in your home, do not disturb the possible asbestos containing material.
    • Generally, if the ACM (asbestos containing material) is in good condition and left in place, it should not present health risks.
    • However, if you are demolishing, renovating, or remodeling your home, you should consider hiring an asbestos inspector.
    • If it is determined that the suspected material does contain asbestos and the best course of action would be removal, hire a professional contractor who is licensed and trained.
