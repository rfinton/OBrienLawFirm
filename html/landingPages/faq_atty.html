What types of attorneys do you work with and receive referrals from? What type of unions do you work with?

We regularly receive referrals from firms of all sizes and types, including:

  • Solo practitioners
  • Small, general practice firms
  • Premier personal injury practices
  • Asbestos plaintiff firms practicing in other areas of the country

We represent anyone who gets diagnosed with asbestos disease, but generally our clients come from an industrial background. We regularly represent members of the Heat and Frost Insulators, Operating Engineers, Pipefitters, and Sheet Metal Workers unions. Other common occupations where exposure occurs include carpenters, electricians, plumbers, boilermakers, and plastics workers, to name a few.
